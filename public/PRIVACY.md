# Privacy Statement

Syncwealth Sdn Bhd (“Company”, “we”, “us” or “our”) is the author and publisher of the internet resource https://mailextract.ilyf.io/ (“Website”), as well as the software application iLyFExtract (“Application”). We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right.

This Privacy Statement ("Statement") describes our localized approach to data. Unlike traditional cloud services, iLyFExtract is designed to ensure your data never leaves your device.

## I. ON-DEVICE SECURITY & LOCAL PROCESSING
The Application functions as a localized utility for .mbox files. Our technical architecture is built on the following principles:

*   **Local Processing**: All viewing, searching, and extraction of email data happens entirely on your iPhone, iPad, Android, or desktop device. No data is processed on our systems.
*   **No Cloud Storage**: We do not operate servers for your data and do not upload your emails, metadata, or attachments to any cloud environment.
*   **Sandboxed Environment**: The Application operates within a secure sandbox. It cannot access your system Mail accounts, contacts, or other external private data without explicit system-level permission for specific files.
*   **Permissions**: Access is strictly limited to the specific files you choose to import via the system file picker.

## II. ZERO TRACKING POLICY
We have omitted all traditional tracking and monetization methods to ensure absolute privacy:

*   **No User Accounts**: You are not required to create an account or provide an identity to use the Application.
*   **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use marketing SDKs, trackers, or analytics (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) within the Application.
*   **No Data Brokerage**: We do not sell, rent, or share user data, as we do not collect it in the first place.
*   **No Advertising**: The Application does not contain targeted advertising or behavioral tracking.

*(Note: Essential payment details / regional IP lookups to process subscription purchases in-app are described in the EULA and are independent of file extraction data).*

## III. COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA
Because the Application processes data locally, our collection is extremely limited:

**III.1 Information You Provide Directly**: If you contact us for support via email, we will receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

**III.2 Website Usage**: When visiting our Website, we may collect basic technical information (such as IP address and browser type) via standard server logs for security and maintenance purposes only.

## IV. USE & DISCLOSURE OF DATA
*   **Support**: We use your contact information only to provide technical assistance.
*   **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information only if required by a court of law in Malaysia to comply with legal obligations.
*   **Third-Party Service Providers**: We do not share data with third-party vendors for processing, as the Application functions independently of any backend infrastructure.

## V. DATA SECURITY
Since your email data remains on your device, its security is governed by your device's encryption and passcode settings. We recommend utilizing device security features (FaceID/Passcode) to protect the files stored within the Application’s local directory.

## VI. DATA RETENTION
*   **Application Data**: Email data remains in the Application until you choose to delete the file or uninstall the Application.
*   **Support Records**: If you contact us for support, we retain that correspondence only as long as necessary to resolve your issue, typically not exceeding 30 days unless required by Malaysian law.

## VII. YOUR RIGHTS (PDPA COMPLIANCE)
In accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia, you have the right to access and correct any personal data we may hold (specifically, your contact info if you have emailed us). Given that we do not track identities or store your files, we generally do not hold "Personal Data" as defined by the act regarding your email content.
